``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space. The **topology induced by $d$** is the topology_
$$\begin{equation}
    \mc{T}_d\coloneqq\l\{U\in\pow\l(X\r)\mid\fa x\in U,\ex\epsilon>0:B_\epsilon\!\l(x\r)\subseteq U\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
