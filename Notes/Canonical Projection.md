``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set and let $\sim$ be an equivalence relation on $X$. The **canonical projection of $X$ under $\sim$** is the function_
$$\begin{equation}
    \pi_\sim:X\to X/\!\sim\ \ \ \ \textrm{\it{mapping}}\ \ \ \ x\mapsto\l[x\r]_\sim
\end{equation}$$
```
