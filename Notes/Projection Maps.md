``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let_ $\l\{X_i\r\}_{i\in I}$ _be an indexed family. The **projection map onto the $j^\textrm{\it{th}}$ coordinate** is the function_
$$\begin{equation}
    \pi_j:\prod\limits_{i\in I}X_i\to X_j\ \ \ \ \textrm{\it{mapping}}\ \ \ \ f\mapsto f\l(j\r).
\end{equation}$$
```
