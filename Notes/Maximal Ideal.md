``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $I\subset R$ be a proper ideal of a ring $R$. Then $I$ is said to be a **maximal ideal in $R$** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa\textrm{ \it{ideals} }J\subseteq R:\l(I\subseteq J\subseteq R\Rightarrow J=I\lor J=R\r).
\end{equation}$$
```
