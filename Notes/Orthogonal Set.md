title: Definition.

_Let $K$ denote either $\R$ or $\C$ and let $V$ be an inner product space over $K$. An indexed subset_ $\l\{u_i\r\}_{i\in I}\subseteq V$ _is called an **orthogonal set** if $u_i\perp u_j$ for all $i\neq j$._
