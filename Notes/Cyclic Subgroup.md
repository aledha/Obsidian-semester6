``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{G,\ast}$ be a group and fix $g\in G$. The **cyclic subgroup generated by $g$** is the subgroup_
$$\begin{equation}
    \cyclic{g}\coloneqq\l\{h\in G\mid \ex n\in\Z:h=g^n\r\}
\end{equation}$$
_of $G$._

```
