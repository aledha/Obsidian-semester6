``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $K$ be a field and fix $n\in\N^+$. The **vector space of $n\times n$ skew-symmetric matrices over $K$** is the linear subspace_
$$\begin{equation}
    \Skew_n\!\l(K\r)\coloneqq\l\{A\in\mat{n}{K}\mid A^\trans=-A\r\}
\end{equation}$$
_of $\mat{n}{K}$._

```
