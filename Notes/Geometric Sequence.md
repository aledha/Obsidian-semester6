title: Definition.

_Let $K$ denote either $\R$ or $\C$ and fix $a_0,r\in K$. A **geometric sequence** is any sequence_ $\tpl{a_n}_{n\in\N}$ _of the form $a_n\coloneqq a_0r^n$._
