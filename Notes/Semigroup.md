title: Definition.

_Let $S$ be a set and let $\cdot$ be a binary operation on $S$. The pair $\tpl{S,\cdot}$ is said to be a **semigroup** if $\cdot$ is associative; that is, if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x,y,z\in S:\l(x\cdot y\r)\cdot z=x\cdot\l(y\cdot z\r).
\end{equation}$$
