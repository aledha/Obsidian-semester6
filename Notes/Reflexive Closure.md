``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set and let $R$ be a binary relation on $X$. The **reflexive closure of $R$ w.r.t. $X$** is the binary relation_
$$\begin{equation}
    \rfcl R\coloneqq R\cup\id_X.
\end{equation}$$

```
