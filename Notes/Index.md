``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $H\subseteq G$ be a subgroup of a group $G$. The **index of $H$ in $G$**, denoted $\l[G:H\r]$, is the number of left (or right) cosets of $H$ in $G$; that is_
$$\begin{equation}
    \l[G:H\r]\coloneqq\l|\l\{L\in\pow\l(G\r)\mid\ex g\in G:L=gH\r\}\r|.
\end{equation}$$
```
