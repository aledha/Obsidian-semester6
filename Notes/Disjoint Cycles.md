``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $X$ be a set and fix $k,l\in\N^+$. Then two cycles $\sigma\coloneqq\l(a_1\ \cdots a_k\r)$ and $\tau\coloneqq\l(b_1\ \cdots\ b_l\r)$ are said to be **disjoint** if_
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa i\in\l\{1,\dots,k\r\},\fa j\in\l\{1,\dots,l\r\}:a_i\neq b_l.
\end{equation}$$
```
