``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $M$ and $N$ be $R$-modules. An **$R$-module isomorphism from $M$ to $N$** is an invertible $R$-module homomorphism $\phi:M\to N$. That is, $R$-module isomorphisms are isomorphisms in $\catmod$._
```
