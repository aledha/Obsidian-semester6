``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $R$ be a commutative ring and fix $a,b\in R\comp\l\{0\r\}$. Then **$a$ and $b$ are associate** if $a\divides b$ and $b\divides a$, in which case we write $a\sim b$._

```
