``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $R$ be a commutative ring with unit and fix a polynomial $f\in R\l[x\r]$. An element $\alpha\in R$ is said to be a **root of $f$** if_ $f\in\ker\eval_\alpha$_; that is, if_ $\eval_\alpha\!\l(f\r)=0$_._

```
