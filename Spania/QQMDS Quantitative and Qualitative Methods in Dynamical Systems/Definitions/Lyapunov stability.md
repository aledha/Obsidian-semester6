>In simple terms, if the solutions that start out near an equilibrium point $x_e$ stay near $x_e$ forever, then $x_e$ is Lyapunov stable. 

More strongly, if $x_e$ is Lyapunov stable and all solutions that start out near $x_e$ converge to $x_e$, then $x_e$ is said to be asymptotically stable.
