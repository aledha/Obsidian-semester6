``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $\tpl{X,\mc{T}}$ be a topological space. The **set of closed sets of $X$ w.r.t. $\mc{T}$** is the set_
$$\begin{equation}
    \mc{F}\coloneqq\l\{C\in\pow\l(X\r)\mid X\comp C\in\mc{T}\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
