``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

_Let $R$ and $S$ be binary relations. The **composition of $S$ after $R$** is the relation_
$$\begin{equation}
    S\circ R\coloneqq\l\{\tpl{x,z}\mid\ex y\l(xRy\land ySz\r)\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
